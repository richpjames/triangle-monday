Triangle Monday

We aim to create change through film and social content. Responsibly.

Triangle Monday is a creative production company specializing in creative strategy, content production, and climate-positive campaigns. Led by Andrea Walji, an award-winning Executive Producer with 20+ years of experience, we've been crafting films, photography, and impact campaigns since 2017 that uniquely blend environmental and social justice themes with character-driven storytelling and measurable impact.

We understand that the challenges we face aren't isolated issues. That's why we infuse our films with deep insight, layered strategy, and a commitment to driving positive change.

Through visually compelling campaigns, we collaborate with stakeholders, experts, brands, and non-profits to create content that inspires hope, solutions and actions. Our work spans the Sustainable Development Goals, covering topics like illegal pet trade, species awareness, stories of activists and environmental leaders, climate and social justice, natural climate solutions, energy, equality and much more.

Currently, we're focused on the intersection of the climate crisis and global food systems, partnering with key players to drive food system change through powerful storytelling.

From concept development and strategic positioning to scriptwriting, design, and final deliverables, we tailor our approach to each project’s unique needs. Andrea curates expert teams based on the project’s brief, budget, and timeline, all while prioritising sustainability and minimising waste.
